My name is Natalie, but most people call me by my middle name, Grace : ) I just graduated with a Bachelor's and Master's in Computer Science from the University of Washington (go dawgs!), where I researched at the UW Security and Privacy Lab under Yoshi Kohno. I'm interested in applying security and privacy perspectives to understand and mitigate sociotechnical harms of emerging technologies, particularly AI.

Currently, I'm a software engineer at Microsoft, working on their machine learning and AI platforms.
